Danbo International Schools High Achievement for all

Wecome To Danbo International School

You are welcome to our website as we provide you, not only with information about what we do in Danbo International Schools but also how we live up to our vision of providing high achievement for all. Danbo International Schools is a co-educational boarding and day school of international standard. The school was established on March 1981 with branches in Kaduna and Abuja. It is a school of outstanding record with modern boarding and day facilities. For over 38 years, we have been in the business of helping students achieve their potentials.

As a school with 360 degrees educational approach,Our Curriculum strategically covers:

Junior Secondary School Certificate of Education curriculum (JSSCE)

International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE).

West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE)

Cambridge Lower Secondary Checkpoint curriculum

Our Sixthform also prepare our students for the SAT, IGCSE intermediate, TOEFL, IELTS, GRE, GMAT and IJMB. We have presented students with outstanding records in SAT, TOEFL, GCE, WASCE and other external examinations.The Citadel of Learning caters for Young Learners under the various age brackets categorized in the education sector of Nigeria. Our school comprises the Nursery section, Primary section, College section, Danbo Special Education Needs School (DSENS) and the Sixthform College.

Our Sixthform is one of the few Pre- University and A’Level Colleges in the world that offers comprehensive academic programs that Prepare, Remediate and Equip young people with relevant skills in order to they pursue their University Education with ease and focus. Our Nursery section has all the Early education departments Crèche,Playgroup and Pre-School.
