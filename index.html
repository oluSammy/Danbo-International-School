Who We Are

Danbo International Schools is a co-educational boarding and day school of international standard. The school was established on March 1981 with branches in Kaduna and Abuja. It is a school of outstanding record with modern boarding and day facilities. For over 38 years, we have been in the business of helping students achieve their potentials.

As a school with 360 degrees educational approach, we have strategically developed curriculum covering:

  • sendJunior Secondary School Certificate of Education curriculum (JSSCE)
  • sendCambridge Lower Secondary Checkpoint curriculum
  • sendWest African Senior School Certificate Examination curriculum (WASSCE)
  • sendInternational General Certificate of Secondary Education curriculum (IGCSE)
