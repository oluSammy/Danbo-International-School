Danbo International School Abuja

At Danbo, we take pride in our ability to create a unique learning path for each child regardless of where they are on the learning rung because, we believe EVERY CHILD CAN SUCCEED and expect a HIGH ACHIEVEMENT FOR ALL learners. Our learners are inspired to take learning beyond the classroom to solve problems in the real world as global leaders contributing to the success of their environment. Children become CRITICAL THINKERS, PROBLEM SOLVERS, ELOQUENT COMMUNICATORS and EXCELLENT GLOBAL MINDED LEADERS.

FROM THE DESK OF THE HEAD OF SCHOOL

Danbo International School, Abuja is a warm, fun filled and engaging learning environment where we believe that learning flows with excitement and awe moments. Danbo is a family preferred learning brand that has a niche for child-centred learning with digital integration.

DIPSA is born to inspire, instruct and implement standard global practices and world-class education using the framework of STEM curriculum for the Sciences and active participatory teaching and learning methods to develop children with the ability to inquire, communicate, create, innovate, collaborate for success, make excellent choices, reason critically and problem solve.

The incredible range of opportunities provided via curricular and extra — curricular learning helps children develop a wide spectrum of skills and understanding coming from caring professionals and educators.

MR. OBAKA EMMANUEL YUSUF
Principal

OUR MISSION

To build internalized skills in young learners using a child centred, well-balanced curriculum that awakens the core potential of the child and enables him solve problems with a team of professional educators in a caring, disciplined and emotionally safe environment.

As digital natives, ICT is integrated into all aspects of the curriculum, in addition to teaching responsible online behaviours and digital footprints. Music and creativity as a source of harmony in our community is also valued.

OUR VALUES

We are a school that supports diversity, in origin and achievement. Soft skills that enhances learning such as gr it, tenacity, morality, respect, adaptability, communication, diligence, enquiry, cooperation, fairness, dynamism, thoughtfulness and excellence are inculcated throughout the teaching and learning process to help the child function in the society.

Our goal is to give every pupil, the maximum competency each can achieve in the core curriculum, along with a maturity appropriate to their development to benefit from the wider life of our school and the opportunities presented by their next school. The staff strive in equal measure to contribute to and develop ourselves through our school.
