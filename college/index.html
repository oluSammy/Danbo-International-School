It is balanced, relevant and differentiated to match the needs, abilities and aspirations of our students. ADMISSION

Danbo International college is a co-educational boarding and day school. To be eligible for admission into year 7 ( JS1) candidate must be 11years old by the year of admission. We also make provision for transfer students. All prospective candidates under-go entrance examination. Examination locations and dates are usually communicated as at when due.

Application form cost N10,000.00 (ten thousand naira) only. Every fresh applicant should note the following:
1. obtain or fill an on-line or hard copy " Application form" with all necessary information
2. all candidates are to come to the examination venue with the examination slip which will serve as entrance slip to the examination centre
3. successful candidates are expected to provide the following documents:

  • A) photocopy of candidate's birth certificate
  • B) 2 recent passport photograph ( preferably in current school's uniform)
  • C) the most recent Academic Report from candidate's current school
  • D) parents living outside Kaduna to provide name and contact details of a Guardian living in Kaduna where possible

4. A provisional letter of acceptance will be issued to successful candidates only 5. The following documents will be given to successful candidates

  • A) medical form
  • B) check-in list for boys and girls
  • C) list of recommended books
  • D) school bill ( pay advice)
  • E) parents and student data form
  • F) rules and regulations booklets and consent form to be signed by both parents and child
