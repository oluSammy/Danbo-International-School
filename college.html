PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE

It is my pleasure to welcome you to our School website and we thank you for taking the time to visit. We hope to provide you with some useful information about our school and to give you an insight into the wealth of experience we offer our students over 39years. We are located in Kubani Crescent, Barnawa GRA Kaduna South Local Government, Kaduna State.

We create a safe, nurturing yet challenging environment that is built on positive relationship, developing the whole student- emotional, socially and academically. We care for and appreciate each of our students as the unique individuals that they are, each with distinct talents, interests and needs, and each with differing learning styles.

Danbo International College prepares students for a 21st century world. We provide an appealing and stimulating curriculum that is flexible and tailored to the needs of each student as we strive for high educational outcomes. We use innovative techniques, rich resources, proven instructional strategies and engaging programmes to promote students learning.

Our Curriculum...

Our curriculum provides the students with the skills, knowledge, experience and imaginative understanding for a lifetime of learning and enjoyment, as well as an awareness and respect for moral values and the community.

The curriculum is designed to inspire and challenge our students at every level in the school. It is ambitious and encouraging depth of exploration. It is balanced, relevant and differentiated to match the needs, abilities and aspirations of our students. It promotes the spiritual, moral, social, cultural, mental and physical development of each student and prepares students for the opportunities, responsibilities and experiences of adult life.
